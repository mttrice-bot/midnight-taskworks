# 10 UGC Video Script Templates

## Template 1: "Honest Review" Style
**Hook (0-3s):** "I've been using [product] for [timeframe] and here's what I actually think..."
**Body (3-30s):** Show product, demonstrate key features, mention 2-3 pros
**CTA (30-40s):** "If you're looking for [result], this is worth trying. Link in bio."

## Template 2: "Before & After" Style
**Hook (0-3s):** "Here's what my [situation] looked like before vs after using [product]..."
**Body (3-25s):** Show before scenario, then after, highlight the transformation
**CTA (25-35s):** "This changed everything for me. Check the link."

## Template 3: "Problem Solver" Style
**Hook (0-3s):** "Stop doing [common mistake]. Do this instead."
**Body (3-25s):** Show the problem, demonstrate product as solution
**CTA (25-35s):** "Trust me, your future self will thank you. Link below."

## Template 4: "Day in the Life" Style
**Hook (0-3s):** "Come with me while I [activity] using [product]"
**Body (3-40s):** Show product integrated into daily routine
**CTA (40-50s):** "This makes my life so much easier. Get yours here."

## Template 5: "Unboxing/First Impressions"
**Hook (0-3s):** "Just got [product] in the mail — let's see if it's worth the hype."
**Body (3-35s):** Unbox, set up, first impressions, pros/cons
**CTA (35-45s):** "Should you buy it? I'll let you decide. Link below."

## Template 6: "Tutorial/How-To"
**Hook (0-3s):** "Here's exactly how to [achieve result] in under 5 minutes."
**Body (3-30s):** Step-by-step tutorial using the product
**CTA (30-40s):** "Save this for later and grab the product at the link."

## Template 7: "Comparison" Style
**Hook (0-3s):** "[Product A] vs [Product B] — which one should you buy?"
**Body (3-35s):** Side-by-side comparison, highlight key differences
**CTA (35-45s):** "My recommendation? [Winner]. Link here."

## Template 8: "Results" Style
**Hook (0-3s):** "I used [product] for [timeframe] and here's the result."
**Body (3-25s):** Show results/metrics, before and after
**CTA (25-35s):** "Results speak for themselves. Try it here."

## Template 9: "Storytime" Style
**Hook (0-3s):** "So I bought [product] and honestly? I wasn't expecting this..."
**Body (3-30s):** Tell a short story, build curiosity, reveal product benefit
**CTA (30-40s):** "Moral of the story — just get it. Link in bio."

## Template 10: "What's In My Bag/Favorites"
**Hook (0-3s):** "My current favorite [category] products that I'm obsessed with."
**Body (3-30s):** Show 3-5 products, spend most time on the featured one
**CTA (30-40s):** "This one's my absolute favorite. Grab it here."
