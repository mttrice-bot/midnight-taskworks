# AI Prompts for Sales Professionals — 30 Power Prompts

## Prospecting
1. "Write a cold email to [title] at [company type] about [offer]. Keep it under 100 words."
2. "Create a 5-touch prospecting sequence for [target]. Mix email and social touches."
3. "Write a LinkedIn connection request message that gets accepted."
4. "Draft a voicemail script that gets callbacks. 20 seconds max."
5. "Generate 10 subject lines for a cold outreach campaign about [product]."

## Discovery & Qualification
6. "Write 5 discovery questions for a sales call about [product/service]."
7. "Create a BANT qualification framework script (Budget, Authority, Need, Timeline)."
8. "Draft a 'why now' question that creates urgency without pressure."
9. "Write a discovery call follow-up email that summarizes and sets next steps."
10. "Create a disqualification script for when a prospect isn't a good fit."

## Presentations & Demos
11. "Write a 5-minute product demo script. Hook → Problem → Solution → Proof → Close."
12. "Create 3 different elevator pitches for [product]. 30 seconds, 60 seconds, 2 minutes."
13. "Draft a proposal summary email. 3 bullet points maximum."
14. "Write a case study summary template. Problem → Solution → Results."
15. "Create a comparison script: our product vs [competitor]. Focus on our strengths."

## Closing
16. "Write 5 assumptive close scripts for different scenarios."
17. "Draft a 'we're running out of inventory' urgency email that's honest, not manipulative."
18. "Create a next-step email that makes it easy to say yes."
19. "Write a script for handling the objection: 'I need to think about it.'"
20. "Draft a 'lost deal' follow-up email that keeps the door open."
