# AI Prompts for Real Estate Agents — 30 Power Prompts

## Listing Descriptions
1. "Write a compelling MLS listing for a [bedroom]bd/[bath]ba home in [neighborhood]. Highlight: [key features]. Target buyer: [demographic]."
2. "Rewrite this listing to be more emotionally compelling: [paste listing]. Focus on lifestyle benefits."
3. "Create 3 different listing headlines for a [property type]. One luxury, one family-friendly, one investment-focused."

## Client Communications
4. "Write a follow-up email to a client who viewed [property]. They seemed interested but hesitant. Address their likely objections."
5. "Draft a script for handling the objection: 'I think we should wait for prices to drop.'"
6. "Write a warm introduction email to a new potential seller in [neighborhood]. Reference recent sales in their area."

## Marketing & Social Media
7. "Write 5 social media captions for [property]. One for Instagram, one for Facebook, one for LinkedIn."
8. "Create a 7-day content plan for a real estate agent specializing in [neighborhood/market]."
9. "Write an open house invitation text message. Create urgency without being pushy."

## Market Analysis
10. "Analyze this market data: [paste data]. Identify 3 key trends and what they mean for buyers."
11. "Write a quarterly market update email to my database. Include [specific stats]."
12. "Create a comparative market analysis summary for [address]. Key selling points vs recent comps."

## Negotiation & Closing
13. "Write a counter-offer email that keeps negotiations positive while protecting my client's interests."
14. "Draft a script for when a home inspection reveals [issue]. Advise client on next steps."
15. "Write a 'congratulations on your offer' message that also prepares them for next steps."

## Business Growth
16. "Create a 30-day lead generation plan for a new agent with a [$X] marketing budget."
17. "Write a script for a cold call to expired listings in [area]."
18. "Draft a partnership proposal email to [local business type] for cross-referrals."
19. "Create a sphere of influence outreach sequence. 5 emails over 2 weeks."
20. "Write a script for asking for referrals from past clients without feeling awkward."
