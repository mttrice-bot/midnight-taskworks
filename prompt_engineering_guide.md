# ChatGPT Prompt Engineering Guide — 50 Advanced Prompts

## The 5 Prompt Frameworks

### Framework 1: Role Prompting
"Act as a [expert role]. Your task is to [task]. Use your expertise to [specific outcome]."
Example: "Act as a senior copywriter. Your task is to write 5 headlines for a productivity app. Use your expertise to make them emotionally compelling and action-oriented."

### Framework 2: Chain of Thought
"Walk through this step by step. First [step 1], then [step 2], then [step 3]."
Example: "Walk through this step by step. First analyze the customer's pain points, then identify the emotional triggers, then write the email body."

### Framework 3: Format Constraint
"Give me [number] [type] about [topic]. Format as [specific format]."
Example: "Give me 10 tweet ideas about time management. Format as a numbered list with one sentence each."

### Framework 4: Persona + Context
"You are [persona] talking to [audience]. The context is [situation]."
Example: "You are a seasoned entrepreneur talking to a room full of college students. The context is a 5-minute talk about starting a business with no money."

### Framework 5: Iterative Refinement
"Here's my first draft: [draft]. Please [specific improvement]. Version 2: [improved]. Now [further refinement]."

## 10 Prompts for Content Creation
1. "Write a viral Twitter thread about [topic]. Hook should be controversial. End with a surprising insight."
2. "Give me 10 blog post titles about [topic] that use the 'curiosity gap' technique."
3. "Rewrite this paragraph in 5 different tones: professional, casual, persuasive, humorous, urgent."
4. "Create a 30-day content calendar for [niche]. Include post ideas, formats, and best posting times."
5. "Write a LinkedIn post that positions me as an expert in [field]. Include a personal story."

## 10 Prompts for Business & Sales
6. "Write a cold email template for reaching out to [target] about [offer]. Keep it under 100 words."
7. "Create 5 objection-handling scripts for [product/service]. Each should acknowledge the concern and reframe it."
8. "Write a sales page outline for [product]. Use PAS framework: Problem, Agitate, Solve."
9. "Generate 10 FAQ questions and answers about [topic]. Answers should be clear and benefit-focused."
10. "Write a follow-up sequence for [situation]. 3 emails, spaced 3 days apart."

## 10 Prompts for Productivity
11. "Break down [goal] into daily tasks for the next 30 days. Prioritize by impact."
12. "Create a SOP (standard operating procedure) for [task]. Include steps, tools, and time estimates."
13. "Analyze my schedule: [paste schedule]. Identify time blocks I could optimize."
14. "Write a meeting agenda template for [meeting type]. Include time allocations."
15. "Create a decision-making framework for [situation]. Include criteria and weighting."

## 10 Prompts for Learning & Research
16. "Explain [concept] like I'm 10 years old. Then explain it like I'm a college student."
17. "Create a study guide for [topic]. Include key concepts, examples, and practice questions."
18. "Summarize [article/text] in 3 bullet points at 3 levels: beginner, intermediate, advanced."
19. "Compare and contrast [topic A] vs [topic B]. Use a table format."
20. "Create a mind map outline for [subject]. Include main branches and sub-topics."

## 10 Prompts for Technical & Coding
21. "Write a Python function that [task]. Include error handling and comments."
22. "Debug this code: [paste code]. Explain what's wrong and show the fix."
23. "Explain [technical concept] to a non-technical stakeholder."
24. "Write a README for a project that [description]. Include setup, usage, and examples."
25. "Generate test cases for a function that [description]. Include edge cases."

## Bonus: The Ultimate Prompt Template
