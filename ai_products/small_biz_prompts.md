# AI Prompts for Small Business Owners — 30 Power Prompts

## Marketing & Content
1. "Write 5 Facebook post ideas for my [business type] this week. Each should solve a specific customer problem."
2. "Create a 30-day social media content calendar for a [business type] in [city]."
3. "Write an About Us page that tells our story and connects with customers emotionally."
4. "Generate 10 blog post titles for a [business type] targeting [customer type]."
5. "Write a Google My Business post announcing [news/promotion]."

## Customer Service
6. "Write a response template for a 3-star review mentioning [issue]. Turn it into a positive."
7. "Draft a script for handling an upset customer on the phone. Keep it calm and solution-focused."
8. "Write an FAQ document for my [product/service]. Cover the top 10 questions."
9. "Create a follow-up email sequence for new customers. 3 emails over 2 weeks."
10. "Write a thank you message that encourages repeat business."

## Operations & Productivity
11. "Create a standard operating procedure for [task]. Include steps, tools, and time estimates."
12. "Draft an employee handbook section on [policy]. Keep it clear and professional."
13. "Write a job description for [role] at a small [business type]."
14. "Create a weekly checklist template for [role/department]."
15. "Write an email template for requesting payment from overdue invoices."

## Sales & Growth
16. "Write a sales script for [product/service]. Handle the top 3 objections."
17. "Create a 5-email nurture sequence for leads who downloaded our free [lead magnet]."
18. "Draft a partnership proposal to send to [complementary business]."
19. "Write a referral request email that doesn't feel awkward."
20. "Create a pricing page outline for [product/service]. Justify the value."
