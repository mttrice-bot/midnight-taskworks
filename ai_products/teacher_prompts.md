# AI Prompts for Teachers — 30 Power Prompts

## Lesson Planning
1. "Create a 45-minute lesson plan for [grade] students on [topic]. Include hook, activity, and assessment."
2. "Write 5 discussion questions about [topic] that promote critical thinking."
3. "Generate a quiz with 10 questions about [topic]. Include answer key."
4. "Create a group activity where students [learning goal]. Include roles and rubric."
5. "Write a creative homework assignment for [topic] that doesn't feel like busy work."

## Classroom Management
6. "Draft a classroom management plan for [grade level]. Include rules, consequences, and rewards."
7. "Write a script for addressing [behavior issue] with a student privately."
8. "Create a parent communication template for [situation: good news/concern/update]."
9. "Write a first-day-of-school welcome letter to parents."
10. "Draft a substitute teacher plan for a [subject] class. Include schedule and key info."

## Differentiation & Support
11. "Modify this lesson for an advanced student: [paste lesson]. Make it more challenging."
12. "Create a simplified version of [concept] for struggling learners. Use analogies."
13. "Write 3 extension activities for students who finish early."
14. "Create an IEP-friendly version of [assignment]. Reduce complexity while maintaining rigor."
15. "Design a choice board for [topic] with 9 different activity options."

## Assessment & Feedback
16. "Write a rubric for assessing [project/assignment]. Include 4 levels of mastery."
17. "Create 3 different assessment formats for [topic]: multiple choice, short answer, project."
18. "Write feedback comments for a student who [specific performance]. Be encouraging and specific."
19. "Design a self-assessment checklist for students working on [project]."
20. "Create a peer review template for [assignment type]."
