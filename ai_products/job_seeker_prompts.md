# AI Prompts for Job Seekers — 30 Power Prompts

## Resume & Cover Letter
1. "Rewrite this bullet point to be more achievement-focused: [paste bullet]. Use metrics where possible."
2. "Create a professional summary for my resume. I'm a [role] with [years] experience in [industry]."
3. "Write a cover letter for [job title] at [company]. Highlight my experience with [key skill]."
4. "Tailor my resume for a [specific job title] role. Emphasize [relevant skills]."
5. "Rewrite my resume to pass ATS screening. Use keywords from this job description: [paste JD]."

## Interview Prep
6. "Write 5 answers to 'Tell me about yourself' for [role]. Each 60 seconds."
7. "Create STAR method answers for: [paste common interview question]."
8. "Prepare answers to 10 common behavioral questions for [industry]."
9. "Write 3 good questions to ask the interviewer about [company/role]."
10. "Draft a follow-up thank you email after an interview. Mention [specific topic discussed]."

## Networking
11. "Write a LinkedIn message to someone at [target company] asking for an informational interview."
12. "Create a LinkedIn profile headline that gets me noticed by recruiters in [industry]."
13. "Draft a post announcing I'm looking for a [role] position. Professional and authentic."
14. "Write a networking email to reconnect with a former colleague."
15. "Create a 30-60-90 day plan to discuss in interviews for [role]."

## Career Growth
16. "Identify 3 career paths for someone with my skills: [list skills]. Include salary ranges."
17. "Create a skill development plan for transitioning from [current role] to [target role]."
18. "Write a salary negotiation script. I want to ask for [amount]. Justify it."
19. "Draft a resignation letter that maintains good relationships."
20. "Create a personal brand statement for my career in [industry]."
